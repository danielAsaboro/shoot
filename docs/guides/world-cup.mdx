The World Cup is a seasonal knockout tournament that runs alongside rolling prop challenges. It provides the highest-stakes competitive format on the platform.

## Tournament Structure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Division Selection">
    4 divisions based on asset class: **Crypto Cup**, **Metals Cup**, **Energy
    Cup**, **Forex Cup**. Traders choose their division at registration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Qualifying Phase (2 weeks)">
    Top N traders per division by composite RAROI score qualify for the knockout
    bracket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Group Stage">
    32 qualifiers per division are drawn into 8 groups of 4. Round-robin within
    groups over 3 matchdays. Top 2 per group advance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Knockout Phase (4 rounds)">
    Single-elimination bracket. Head-to-head RAROI comparison per match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grand Finals">
    Division champions compete in a 48-hour all-markets battle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scoring: RAROI

World Cup uses **RAROI** (Risk-Adjusted ROI) instead of the additive tournament score. See [Scoring Engine](/guides/scoring-engine#raroi-world-cup) for the full formula.

RAROI is multiplicative — it produces clear "who is the better trader" signals for 1v1 matches, unlike the tournament score which is optimized for cohort-wide rankings.

## Group Draw Algorithm

The draw is **seeded and deterministic** — same inputs always produce the same groups.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Seed Qualifiers">
    Top 32 qualifiers ranked by RAROI from qualifying phase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create 4 Pots">
    - **Pot 1** (Seeds 1–8): Strongest traders - **Pot 2** (Seeds 9–16) - **Pot
    3** (Seeds 17–24) - **Pot 4** (Seeds 25–32): Weakest qualifiers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seeded Shuffle">Each pot is shuffled using the seeded RNG.</Step>
  <Step title="Distribution">
    One trader from each pot placed into each of 8 groups. This ensures every
    group has a mix of skill levels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Group of Death Check">
    If no group has an average seed strength in the top quartile, the algorithm
    swaps Pot 2 traders to strengthen the weakest group — guaranteeing at least
    one "Group of Death" for narrative drama.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redemption Bracket

Semi-final losers aren't eliminated — they're paired in a separate redemption bracket.

- The redemption winner earns the **"Comeback"** badge
- They re-enter the season point pool
- This keeps eliminated traders engaged instead of churning

In the alpha test, **64%** of eliminated traders joined the Redemption Bracket (target was 40%).

## Match Resolution

Each World Cup match is resolved by comparing RAROI scores over the match window:

1. Compute RAROI for both traders over the match period
2. Apply any active risk event modifiers
3. Higher RAROI wins
4. If RAROI difference \< 0.5 points: draw (extra time rules apply)

## Season Flow

```
Registration → Qualifying (2 weeks) → Group Draw → Group Stage (3 matchdays)
    → Round of 16 → Quarter Finals → Semi Finals
        → Finals (winners)
        → Redemption Bracket (semi-final losers)
    → Grand Finals (division champions)
```

## Division-Specific Markets

| Division   | Allowed Markets                |
| ---------- | ------------------------------ |
| Crypto Cup | BTC, ETH, SOL, BONK            |
| Metals Cup | XAU, XAG                       |
| Energy Cup | WTI, BRENT                     |
| Forex Cup  | EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY, AUDUSD |

Trading outside your division's markets during World Cup matches results in disqualification with a `specialist_violation` flag.
